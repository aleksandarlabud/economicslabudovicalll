Economics in Yugioh

Yu-Gi-Oh is a trading card game in which two players attempt to defeat each other by decreasing their opponent's Life Points using their decks of cards. Beside the actual game the trading aspect and secondary market are both huge aspects of the game. Yugioh can be used as a great example of how a full scale economy works. Law of Demand, Law of Supply, Scarcity, Determinants of Demand, Inferior and Goods, and Surplus, are all important economic terms which can apply to Yu-Gi-Oh. However, to understand how those work, some terms must be defined from the game of Yu-gi-oh.
